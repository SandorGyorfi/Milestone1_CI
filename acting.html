Acting


In 2017, following his success on RTL, he was given the opportunity to take part in an image film for one of the largest pharmaceutical companies in Hungary (Richter Gedeon Pharmaceuticals). (Az elismerés minden nőnek jár! - YouTube, Director: Dávid Spáh)



In 2019, she was cast in the lead role of one of the children (Hanna) in the new daily fiction series 'Sres. Papis/Dear Daddie’ dramedy on the leading commercial channel TV2, which runs in prime time, and played the role for three seasons (2019-2021).
